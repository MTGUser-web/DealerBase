Administrator

Accounts
Point of contact for all financial matters including billing, rates, statements, and invoices.
Leads User

Stock Manager
Manages vehicle inventory including adding, editing, deleting vehicles, images, and appraisals.
Sales
Contact details shown on listings. Email inquiries go to all listed sales contacts.
